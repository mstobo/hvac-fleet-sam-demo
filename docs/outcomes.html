How it flows (business view)

Ingest

Fleet sensors publish to Solace at high frequency.

Filter

Suppress unchanged readings; forward meaningful change.

Summarize

Human-readable rollups stored for trends and tools.

Detect

Rule-based alerts and fleet status — no model required.

Act (on demand)

SAM agents, Slack, dashboards when operators or automation need insight.
